Our Heritage

Pune to the World

Established in the industrial heartland of Pune, Shourex Global began with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between Indian agricultural prowess and the sophisticated requirements of the global market. Our journey is rooted in the rich traditions of Maharashtra's commerce, infused with modern logistics technology.

What started as a specialized export house for regional spices has transformed into a multifaceted global entity. Today, we handle complex supply chains for fresh produce, frozen seafood, and essential commodities, ensuring that every shipment carries the hallmark of Indian reliability and international quality standards.
